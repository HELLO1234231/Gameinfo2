GAME REVIEW

FORTNITE

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in three distinct game mode versions that otherwise share the same general gameplay and game engine . Initial release date was on 21 July 2017. Designer Was Darren Sugg. Developers are Epic Games, People Can Fly. Fortnite used to be cross platform but now it is not but if your a console player you're lucky. The game has many genres such as Battle royale game, Survival game, Third-person shooter, Sandbox, Action role-playing game, Nonlinear gameplay.
