GAME REVIEW

Minecraft

Minecraft is a very good game you can play with your friends and family. It is a good game to kill time. You can build whatever you want whenever you want. It is a game about survival you can get resources and food. You can get diamond, iron, gold etc. It is your block world your choice. You can buy skins and mods and you can make your own skins. You can join servers and play bed wars, cake wars and sky block. But be careful there are skeletons Zombies spiders you name it they will be on the hunt and there are bosses to fight to complete this game.Whilst playing this game I experienced a lot I killed a boss known as the wither rarely anyone unlock this achievement killing it is super hard even if you have the best gear, it is very rare you defeat that boss including the ender dragon. Overall, this game gets a SOLID 9/10.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a very interesting game believe it or not it has a timeline. You can play with your friends and family. It is a Player vs player game in each match there is 100 players at max. You can also play duos this game mode is normal, but you are in teams of two you can also choose if you want to play solo in duos. Squads is a game mode where there are teams of four instead and you can also play solo in that game mode, but it will be quite hard to survive as this time you will be fighting four people if you encounter a squad. There is also creative mode where you can play games made by the Fortnite community. Every now and then Fortnite adds special limited game modes which is fun to play in. You can buy skins and buy a battle Pass.There once was a game mode that came in Halloween that had zombies and a boss known as Storm king. If you are lucky enough you can join live events which mostly are very cool. Overall, this game gets a SOLID 8/10.

You basically must vote the imposter of the ship or complete all the task to win. Whereas the imposter must kill everyone to win. You can change skins before the match starts and your characters skin Colour. C-OPS

c-ops are a Game where you have three game modes deathmatch, defuse and gun game. You can customize your skin and weaponry you can play with your friends and family. Gun game is where you basically must kill to improve your weapons or at least change them, you must kill 15 people to win. Death match is a game mode where you have two teams and you have a specific timer, where the team with the most kills wins. Defuse is where you have two teams and two rounds. One of the rounds you will be defusing bomb that the enemy places and in the second you will be placing the bombs. You get two buy items to kill the enemy team. Overall, I give it a SOLID 6/10.

BRAWL STARS

Brawl Stars is a fun 2D type game. Like many other games it has a battle pass, and you can use tokens, coin and gems to buy things. You can buy skins for characters you own. You get skins in crates every now and then you will get a skin from a crate. You can play Duos or solo. But the lobby fits up to 4 people meaning you can be in the same match, but it will be duos. You can play a game where the team with the most gems wins or a football game mode. There is also one where you must collect as many screws as possible so you can make a boss to defeat the other team whilst they and your team will protect the generator, if you lose it you lose. Overall, I give it a SOLID 5/10.
